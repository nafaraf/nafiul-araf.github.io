Customer Churn EDA and Hypothesis Test

In my analysis, I found that month-to-month contracts and electronic check payment methods are strongly associated with higher churn rates, while longer tenure correlates with lower churn rates. Hypothesis testing confirmed these findings, highlighting the significance of service-related factors in customer retention.
